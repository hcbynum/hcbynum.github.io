Recipe #1 Marsha Knights Homemade Appledoodles


- 2 [&] 2/3’s cups of all purpose flour ( or alternative flour of your choosing )

- 2 tsps. Baking powder - ¼ tsp. salt - ½ tsp. cinnamon - ½ tsp. nutmeg

- ½ cup butter, softened to room temperature

- ½ cup granulated sugar - ½ cup brown sugar

- 2 eggs, slightly beaten ( want them to be a tad frothy )

- 1 tsp. Vanilla or Vanilla extract

- 1 ½ cup chopped apples or shredded

- 3 tbsps. Granulated sugar and cinnamon

- Instructions: Stir flour together with baking powder, salt, the ½ tsp of cinnamon and nutmeg. Softened butter, the ½ cup granulated sugar and brown sugar together. Add eggs and vanilla together. Throughly combine all ingredients except for the apples and 3 tbsps of granulated sugar and cinnamon. Once combined into a soft dough you will add the apple slices or shreds. You can create a small pocket to add in apples or mix them into the dough. With the dough shape into balls with teaspoons then roll into the set aside 3 tbsps of granulated sugar and cinnamon mixture. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, then bake for 12-15 minutes to reach a golden brown AppleDoodle.

 

 

Pumpkin Pie

- 1 Can Pumpkin

- 3 Large Eggs

- 2 tablespoons of Melted Butter

- 1 ½ Cups Brown Sugar

- 2 teaspoons of Cornstarch

- 1 ½ teaspoons of Ground Cinnamon

- 1 ½ teaspoons of Ginger

- ½ teaspoon of freshly ground or grated Nutmeg and Ground Cloves

- 1 teaspoon of All Spice seasoning

- 1 cup Heavy Cream - ½ can Evaporated Milk

- ¼ cup Honey

- 1 teaspoon of All Purpose Flour ( optional to help prevent cracking)

- Instructions: Set aside a premade crust or a store purchased crust for the pie. Start by mixing the baking powder, heavy cream, evaporated milk, honey and flour together gently. Once you have a thick constistensy you will begin adding in the eggs, pumpkin, melted butter, brown sugar and cornstarch. Adding in lastly the cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, ground cloves, and all spice seasonings. Once you have a smooth pale orange consistensy you will be able to pour the pumpkin mix into the crust. Once you have set your pumpkin mix in the crust you can begin to bake the pie. Preheat oven to 375 degrees, then bake. Bake the pie for 45-55 minutes until the outter layers and the center are firm. Confirm the pie is finished baking by sticking a fork or scewer into the pie and confirming it does not stick onto the object.

Sugar Free Banana Creme Cake

-Pillsbury Sugar Free Devil's Food Cake Mix 16 oz

-Smucker's Sugar Free Caramel Sundae Syrup 19.25 oz Smuckers

-1 Banana

-Cool Whip Zero Sugar Whipped Cream Topping (or your choice of sugar free icing)

-Instructions: Preheat oven to 350F. Grease 13x9 cake pan. In a bowl, combine cake mix following directions on bake of cake box to add egg, oil and water/milk. Fold together for two minutes. Pour batter into cake pan. Bake for 30-38 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let cool completely. Chop the banana into slices. Frost the cake with the sugar free whipped cream when completely cooled, add the chopped banana, as well as drizzle to your liking with the sugar free caramel syrup. Enjoy!
